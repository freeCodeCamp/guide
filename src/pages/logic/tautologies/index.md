## Tautologies

**Tautology** is a proposition that is always true, no matter what are the truth values of the propositional variables in it.
For example, p ∨ ¬p is tautology:
|  p |  ¬p  |  p ∨ ¬p  |
|----|------|----------|
| T  |   F  |      T   |
| F  |   T  |      T   |
