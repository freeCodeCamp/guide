## Definition of Mean

 In mathematics, mean is the average number of the total.
 To find the mean, you add all the numbers and divide by how many there are.
 
 For example: Find the mean of: 2, 4, 5, 7, 8.
 
 Method:
 2+4+5+7+8 = 26.
 26 divided by 5 = 5.2
 Mean = 5.2.
