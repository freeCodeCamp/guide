## Area of a Triangle

The area of a triangle is given by 
    A=(1/2) * b * h           
where b= length of base and h= length of the height of the triangle.

When, we have only sides of triangle(say a,b,c) only, we use the formula :

S=(s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c))^1(1/2) 

where s=(a+b+c)/2 .
